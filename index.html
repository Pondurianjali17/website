FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common insights regarding enterprise delivery pipelines and SLA frameworks.

What SLA metrics govern your SRE operations?

Our Tier 1 managed systems are governed by a 99.99% (four-nines) availability agreement. High-priority (P1) incidents receive a 15-minute response SLA, monitored continuously by our distributed operations centers.

Do you support hybrid-cloud deployment topologies?

Yes. We regularly design and manage hybrid systems that interface legacy private infrastructure with hyperscalers. We use container isolation (Kubernetes) and secure endpoints to keep traffic encrypted transit-wide.

How do you approach legacy system modernizations?

We deploy the Strangler Fig pattern to extract services incrementally. This mitigates operational risks, allowing components to modernize while maintaining absolute operational parity between the old and new clusters.
